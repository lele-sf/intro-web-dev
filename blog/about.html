Here are some cool facts about cats:

  • A group of cats is called a clowder.
  • Cats can make over 100 different sounds, while dogs can make only about 10.
  • A cat's nose pad is unique, like a human fingerprint.
  • The average cat spends about two-thirds of its life asleep.
  • Cats have a special collar bone that allows them to always land on their feet when they fall. This is called the "righting reflex."
